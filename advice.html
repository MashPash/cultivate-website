Gardening Tips Schedule by Season

Season Plant Type Care Tips
Spring Flowers Prune and plant new seedlings. Start fertilizing as growth resumes.
Spring Vegetables Plant cool-season crops like peas, lettuce, and spinach. Watch for frost.
Spring Herbs Start sowing seeds indoors or directly in the garden. Ensure good sunlight.
Summer Flowers Water frequently and deadhead blooms to promote new growth.
Summer Vegetables Water deeply and consistently. Harvest summer crops like tomatoes and peppers.
Summer Trees & Shrubs Prune lightly and ensure adequate water during heatwaves.
Fall Perennials Cut back spent foliage. Divide and transplant if necessary.
Fall Bulbs Plant spring-flowering bulbs like tulips and daffodils before the first frost.
Fall Vegetables Plant cool-weather crops. Harvest remaining summer crops.
Winter Indoor Plants Reduce watering. Ensure they receive adequate indirect light.
Winter Trees & Shrubs Mulch to protect roots from frost. Prune dormant trees.
Winter Succulents Keep in dry, sunny areas and water sparingly during cold months.
